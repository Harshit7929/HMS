We provide safe, comfortable, and modern hostels for boys and girls. Each hostel is designed to create a vibrant community while ensuring privacy and comfort for all residents.

Vedavathi Boys Hostel

Vedavathi Hostel offers state-of-the-art facilities with spacious rooms and modern amenities. Ideal for students and professionals, the hostel is located close to academic institutions and provides a serene environment for focused living.

Ganga Boys Hostel

Ganga Boys Hostel is designed for comfort and community living. The hostel features well-furnished rooms, 24/7 security, and recreational areas to ensure residents feel at home. It is an excellent choice for long-term stays.

Narmadha Girls Hostel

Narmadha Hostel is a secure and welcoming place for girls. With its beautifully maintained premises, the hostel provides a safe and nurturing environment. It is equipped with CCTV, biometric access, and other modern facilities.

Krishna Girls Hostel

Krishna Hostel offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience for female residents. The hostel boasts a modern kitchen, spacious rooms, and well-maintained study areas, making it an excellent choice for students.
